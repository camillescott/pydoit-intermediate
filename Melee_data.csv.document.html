Agent Performance Similarity in a Bounded Sakurai Space

The bounded Sakurai Space is a two-dimensional platform-based environment populated by particularly whimsical mathematical agents. These agents compete with each other through a variety of very sciency mechanisms, most of which are outside the bounds of this one-paragraph publication.
